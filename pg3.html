howling wraiths


The last spell howling wraiths is located in the ancestral mound in fog canyon left of queen station and it is a spell that shoots a triangular beam upwards and does about 13 damage but can be upgraded in as soon as you go to the abyss if you move to the left and use the spell on the pedestal and the upgraded version is called abyssal shriek.
